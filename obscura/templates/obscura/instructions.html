INSTRUCTIONS


  • There are 21 questions to be answered- 7 easy, 7 medium and 7 hard.
  • The main goal is to do this within the least time with maximum points and hearts left at the end of it.
  • You start off with 25 points and 3 hearts.
  • You earn points by answering questions. Easy ones give you 25 points, medium ones give you 40 points and hard questions give you 60 points.
  • DO NOT use capital letters to answer!
  • But everything comes with a cost! So everytime you submit a wrong solution, you lose 1 heart.
  • Fret not, for you can earn hearts by playing any of the minigames. You get 1 heart everytime you play a minigame. But you need to pay 10 points everytime you do so.
  • Initially only easy questions are unlocked. You may unlock the medium questions by answering all easy questions correctly, and similarly you answer medium questions to unlock hard questions.
  • Hearts can also be used to unlock medium questions(10 hearts deducted) and hard questions(15 hearts deducted).
  • If you run out of both hearts and points, don't worry! Resources will be replenished for everyone at 10 PM on Saturday.
  • 'Nuff said! Get started then!
